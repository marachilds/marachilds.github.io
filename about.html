My Mondays are Red

I have grapheme-color and spatial-sequence synesthesia. Grapheme-color is the association of colors with numbers and letters, whereas spatial-sequence synesthesia is when months of the year and days of the week appear as a three-dimensional map in space. "Tuesday" is blue, the number nine is yellow, and January is at the 11:00 position.
